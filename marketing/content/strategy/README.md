# content/strategy/

Outputs that **plan** what to ship. Editorial calendars, channel mix, pillar topics, cadence.

**What goes here:**
- `MMYY-content-strategy.md` — full content strategy (from `/content-strategy`)
- `MMYY-editorial-calendar.md` — quarterly or monthly publishing calendar
- `MMYY-pillar-{topic}.md` — deep dives on individual content pillars

**Produced by:** `/content-strategy`

**Naming:** `MMYY-topic.md` (e.g., `0526-content-strategy.md`).

**Depends on:** locked positioning, locked messaging, ICP, competitor research. Read those first.

**Feeds:** the execution lane (what gets written, when, by whom). Strategy → execution is the verb chain.
