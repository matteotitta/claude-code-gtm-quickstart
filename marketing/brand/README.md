# brand/

Visual identity (brand kit) + voice (tone of voice). Two parallel artifacts that together define how your brand looks and sounds.

**What goes here:**
- `MMYY-brand-kit.md` — colors, typography, spacing, components, do's and don'ts (from `/brand-guidelines`)
- `MMYY-tov-guidelines.md` — voice patterns, vocabulary, register, do's and don'ts (from `/tov-guidelines`)
- Optional: `screenshots/` subfolder for source images used to extract visual identity
- Update when: rebrand, product expansion into new category, or stakeholder request

**Produced by:** `/brand-guidelines` + `/tov-guidelines`

**Feeds:** every visual deliverable (landing pages, ads, decks — most gated in this pack), every written deliverable (content, copy, email — partially shipped here).
