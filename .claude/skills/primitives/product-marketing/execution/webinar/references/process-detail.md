## Phase 2: Content Development

**2.1 Create content outline** — default 45-min allocation in `references/format-templates.md`.

**2.2 Write speaker prep materials**
- Talking points per section
- Transition phrases
- Q&A anticipation

**2.3 Create run-of-show**

| Time | Action | Owner | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|-------|
| -30 min | Tech check | [Name] | [Details] |
| -5 min | Speakers ready | [Name] | [Details] |
| 0:00 | Go live | [Name] | [Details] |

**2.4 Plan engagement moments**
- Polls (when and what)
- Q&A prompts
- Resource drops
- Live reactions

Phase 2 checkpoint: outline complete, speaker prep ready, run-of-show documented, engagement moments planned.

## Phase 3: Promotion Strategy

**3.1 Build promotion calendar**

| Timing | Channel | Content | Owner |
|--------|---------|---------|-------|
| T-3 weeks | Email #1 | Announcement | [Name] |
| T-2 weeks | LinkedIn | Speaker preview | [Name] |
| T-1 week | Email #2 | Reminder + value | [Name] |
| T-1 day | Email #3 | Final reminder | [Name] |
| T-1 hour | Email #4 | Going live | [Name] |

**3.2 Write promotional copy**
- Registration page copy
- Email invitations (3-4)
- Social posts (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Partner promotion kit (if co-marketing)

**3.3 Design registration page brief**
- Headline + subheadline
- 3-5 bullet points (what they'll learn)
- Speaker bios
- Form fields

**3.4 Plan partner promotion (if applicable)**

| Partner | Promotion | Assets Needed |
|---------|-----------|---------------|
| [Partner] | [Channels] | [Assets] |

Phase 3 checkpoint: calendar complete, all copy written, landing page brief ready, partner assets prepared (if applicable).

## Phase 4: Post-Webinar

**4.1 Design follow-up sequence**

| Email | Timing | Audience | Content |
|-------|--------|----------|---------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Attendees | Recording + resources |
| 2 | Day 0 | No-shows | Recording + FOMO |
| 3 | Day 2 | All | Key takeaway + CTA |
| 4 | Day 5 | Engaged | Demo offer |

**4.2 Plan repurposing**

| Format | Content | Timeline |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Blog post | Key insights | Week 1 |
| LinkedIn posts | Quote cards | Week 1-2 |
| Short clips | Highlights | Week 1 |
| Podcast | Audio extract | Week 2 |

**4.3 Define success metrics review**
- Registration vs. target
- Attendance rate
- Engagement metrics
- Pipeline generated

Phase 4 checkpoint: follow-up sequence written, repurposing plan created, metrics review template ready.
