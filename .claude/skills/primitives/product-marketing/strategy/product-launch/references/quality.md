# Quality — pre-delivery checklist + examples

Run these gates before handing the launch package to the user. Then check the worked example to calibrate "good."

---

## Pre-delivery checklist

### Strategy quality
- [ ] Feature brief complete with all fields
- [ ] Narrative angle includes story arc and hooks
- [ ] Business objectives have measurable targets
- [ ] Launch tier appropriate for scope

### Asset quality
- [ ] All tier-required assets listed
- [ ] Assets trace to messaging framework
- [ ] Placeholders clearly marked
- [ ] No invented metrics or quotes

### Deployment quality
- [ ] Timeline spans T-14 to T+14
- [ ] Owners assigned to all activities
- [ ] Dependencies documented
- [ ] Contingencies planned

---

## What good looks like — Example: Significant feature launch (Archive + Klaviyo)

**Input context:**
```
Feature: Archive + Klaviyo Integration
Category: Significant (new integration)
Launch date: March 15, 2026
```

**Expected output excerpt:**
```markdown
### Narrative Angle
**Big idea:** Archive customers can now automatically trigger Klaviyo flows from creator activity — turning UGC moments into personalized customer journeys.

**Story arc:**
1. **Problem:** Brands manually track creator activity and update email segments by hand
2. **Insight:** The moment a creator posts is the perfect trigger for personalized outreach
3. **Solution:** Archive now syncs creator events directly to Klaviyo as triggers
4. **Proof:** Beta customers saw 3x engagement on creator-triggered flows
5. **Future:** Every creator mention becomes an automated marketing moment
```

**Why this is good:**
- Big idea is specific and value-driven
- Story arc builds from problem to future
- Includes proof point from beta
- Creates urgency and vision

## Anti-examples (what to avoid)

| Bad pattern | Why it's bad | Better approach |
|-------------|--------------|-----------------|
| "We're excited to announce..." | Generic, no value | Lead with customer benefit |
| Launch day only, no pre/post | Misses momentum opportunity | Full T-14 to T+14 timeline |
| No measurable objectives | Can't measure success | Specific targets per objective |
| Same messaging all channels | Ignores channel differences | Channel-adapted hooks |
