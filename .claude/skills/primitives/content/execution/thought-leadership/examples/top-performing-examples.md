## Common Patterns Across Examples

### Structure
- Always open with problem/context before solution
- Use comparison frameworks to define new concepts
- Include quantified proof points where possible
- End with clear reader action

### Voice
- First-person where appropriate
- Specific tools and companies named
- Contrarian or non-obvious angles
- Practical over theoretical

### Credibility
- Historical context (cycles, evolution)
- Real case studies with metrics
- Named co-authors with relevant expertise
- Borrowed frameworks (JTBD, Pyramid Principle)

### Length & Format
- 1,500-3,000 words typical
- Clear section headlines
- Mix of prose and lists
- Visual frameworks where helpful
