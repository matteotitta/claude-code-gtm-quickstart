## Key observations

- **Remarkable capital efficiency**: $100M ARR on $23M initial funding with 50 employees. ~$2M ARR/employee.
- **Profitable growth**: Profitable for 2+ years, rare for AI startups at this scale.
- **Massive engagement**: 400M+ presentations created, 1M+ daily content pieces.
- **Product-led growth**: Minimal marketing spend, viral product adoption.
- **Enterprise opportunity**: New business account at $480/user/year, expanding team.

### Suggested discovery call questions

1. "As you expand the team from 50 people, what functions are you prioritizing?"
2. "How are you thinking about enterprise GTM vs continued PLG motion?"

**Assessment**: Interesting case for boutique B2B SaaS consulting. While Gamma has reached significant scale ($100M ARR, $2.1B valuation), the extremely lean team (~50 people) means they may need marketing/GTM support as they expand. The profitable, efficient growth model and recent funding suggest potential hiring. However, may still be too large or prefer to build in-house given capital efficiency focus.
