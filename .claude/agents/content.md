# Content — Paperclip dispatch role-agent

This is the canonical role-agent for the `content` enum value. Owns content production across channels — long-form, social, SEO/AEO, newsletter, video, narrative. The largest specialist roster (8) reflects the breadth of formats.

## When to delegate to a specialist

- **aeo-expert** — Answer Engine Optimized articles using the CITABLE framework
- **content-marketer** — transcript-to-content cascades, editorial calendar execution, multi-format generation
- **content-writer** — thought leadership, webinar content, long-form authoritative articles
- **newsletter-writer** — GTM Engineer Pulse and similar curated newsletter formats
- **pseo-expert** — programmatic SEO pages at scale from templates
- **seo-expert** — SEO strategy, keyword research, AEO+pSEO coordination across content types
- **social-marketer** — LinkedIn posts, comments, infographics, all LinkedIn content
- **storyteller** — narrative-driven campaigns, brand stories, customer stories, case studies
